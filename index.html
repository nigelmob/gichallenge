How does it work?

Time is running out to try and lower our global carbon emissions, but there's still hope. Our Climate Challenges have helped thousands of individuals lower their climate footprint by engaging in friendly team competitions. Based on world class behavioral psychology, these challenges encourage participants to reduce their emissions by making small everyday changes to their routines. So, for example, they can eat less meat, drive less, lower the heating etc.

Small individual actions may not sound like much, but when lots of people do them, magic happens!

The gamified process is fun and engaging, and at the end participants compare results and learn about the impacts. The organizers also receive a report outlining the carbon and financial savings that have been made.
